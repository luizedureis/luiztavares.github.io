Software Design

The system was designed to handle judicial-driven financial operations, enabling secure and reliable account and fund blocking/unblocking in compliance with legal requirements.

The architecture emphasizes strong consistency, traceability, and fault tolerance, ensuring that every operation is auditable and accurately processed within a distributed environment. Built to support asynchronous workflows, the system leverages messaging to guarantee resilience and controlled execution of critical processes.

Observability was a core aspect of the design, with comprehensive monitoring and logging in place to provide full visibility into system behavior and operational flows. As a contributor, I focused on building reliable services and ensuring end-to-end traceability across all critical operations.
